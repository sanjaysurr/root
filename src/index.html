Distinct Experiences Under One Roof

Tucked away in the ever-bustling lanes of Koramangala, Bangalore's food capital, Roots Fine Dine brings you an unforgettable dining experience. With cafés, breweries, and gourmet kitchens around every corner, Koramangala is a magnet for foodies. And in this culinary paradise, Roots stands out—not just for its ambiance, but for the flavors that stay with you long after the meal.

Whether it's a quiet dinner date, a celebration with family, or a catch-up with friends, food lovers today crave more than just taste—they seek ambience, variety, and comfort. At Roots, we serve all three with elegance.

A Journey Rooted in Flavor, Experience, and Elegance

Welcome to Roots Fine Dine, a multi-cuisine restaurant nestled in the vibrant heart of Koramangala. Spread across three thoughtfully designed spaces—Ground Floor (Fine Dine), First Floor (Mezzanine), and Rooftop, Roots caters to every mood and moment.

With a heritage of over 15+ years, we've delighted thousands of food lovers with carefully crafted dishes from around the world. Our team of seasoned chefs, warm hospitality crew, and sophisticated interiors create a unique blend of global taste and Indian warmth.

Whether you're indulging in Italian, Chinese, Indian, Continental, or Fusion cuisine—Roots is where your cravings find their perfect match. Roots is not just a restaurant—it's a place where memories are plated, one meal at a time.

Explore Our Spaces at Roots

Ground Floor – Elegant Fine Dine

Step into serenity at the Ground Floor of Roots, our signature fine dine area, designed for intimate meals and special occasions. With cozy lighting, elegant décor, and attentive service, this space reflects timeless sophistication. It's ideal for romantic dinners, family outings, or important business lunches.

First Floor – The Mezzanine Lounge

Our first-floor mezzanine is where classy meets casual. This in-between space bridges the elegance of fine dining and the openness of the rooftop. With a modern setup, plush seating, and soft lighting, it's perfect for small groups, brunch meetings, or those who love dining with a view.

Rooftop – Open-Air Dining Under the Stars

There's nothing quite like dining under the stars, and our rooftop restaurant is proof. With a panoramic view of Koramangala's skyline, cool evening breezes, and ambient lighting, the rooftop at Roots is the ultimate spot for weekend hangouts, celebrations, or date nights.
