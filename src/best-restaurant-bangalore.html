The Five-Floor Layout – Something for Everyone

Before we jump into why Roots stands out, let’s quickly look at its well-planned layout:

  • Ground Floor – Roots Restaurant: A warm, elegant fine dining space ideal for family dinners, date nights, or quiet lunches.

  • Rooftop – Roots Alfresco & Events: Dine under the sky or throw your private party with a scenic view.
